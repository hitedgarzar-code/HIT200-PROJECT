================================================================================
                         ARCHITECTURE OVERVIEW
================================================================================

CHECKOUT FLOW
Customer adds items
    ↓
Goes to /checkout
    ↓
Fills address details
    ↓
Clicks "Continue to Payment"
    ↓
Order created in database
    ↓
Selects payment method:
    ├─ PayNow
    │  └─ Enters phone → Payment polling → Confirmation → Order PAID
    │
    └─ Pay Later
       └─ Selects plan → Instalments created → Order PAY_LATER_ACTIVE
            ↓
            Customer can view schedule at /pay-later/[planId]

ORDERS PAGE FLOW
Visit /orders
    ↓
See all customer orders
    ├─ PayNow orders: Show "PayNow" badge, status "paid"
    └─ Pay Later orders: Show "Pay Later" badge, status, link to schedule
        ↓
        Click "View Instalment Schedule"
        ↓
        View /pay-later/[planId]
        ├─ Plan overview cards
        ├─ Progress bar
        ├─ All payment dates and amounts
        └─ Payment status tracking

================================================================================
                         PAYMENT DETAILS
================================================================================

PAYNOW INSTANT PAYMENT
- Provider: PayNow Zimbabwe
- Supported Wallets: EcoCash, OneMoney, InnBucks, Cards
- Processing Time: 30 seconds - 2 minutes
- Success Rate: 95%+ typical
- Transaction Fee: ~2% (PayNow platform fee)
- Order Status: PAID (immediately after confirmation)

PAY LATER INSTALMENTS

3-Month Plan
- Interest: 5%
- Example (ZWL 6,000): Monthly ZWL 2,100, Total ZWL 6,300
- Best For: Small to medium purchases

6-Month Plan
- Interest: 8%
- Example (ZWL 6,000): Monthly ZWL 1,080, Total ZWL 6,480
- Best For: Medium purchases, balanced budget

12-Month Plan
- Interest: 12%
- Example (ZWL 6,000): Monthly ZWL 560, Total ZWL 6,720
- Best For: Large purchases, extended payment

Late Payment
- Fee: 2% per month on overdue amount
- Grace Period: 0 days (due on date)
- Example: ZWL 1,000 due → ZWL 1,020 if 1 month late

Early Settlement
- Allowed: Yes, anytime
- Penalty: None
- Interest: Prorated adjustment
